🔬 Our Revolutionary Technologies

🧪

Enzymatic Fragrance Release

Advanced enzymes activate upon contact with sweat components, triggering encapsulated fragrance precursors to convert into aromatic compounds through biochemical reactions.

🌡️

pH-Activated Capsules

Smart microcapsules respond to sweat's acidic pH (4-6), dissolving to release carefully curated fragrance oils exactly when your body needs them most.

🌬️

Thermo-Responsive Release

Temperature-sensitive hydrogels melt when body heat increases during sweating, creating a controlled release of volatile essential oils.

🧪

Smart Polymer Matrix

Moisture-sensitive polymers form a protective film that swells upon sweat contact, slowly releasing encapsulated scent molecules for long-lasting fragrance.

🦠

Probiotic Fragrance Production

Engineered skin-safe probiotics metabolize sweat components into pleasant-smelling esters and compounds, naturally transforming your body's chemistry.

⚗️

Adaptive Scent Masking

Cyclodextrin-based odor neutralizers activate with sweat intensity, releasing adaptive fragrances that match your activity level and environment.
